Learn to assess market opportunities, create farm production plans, manage finances, and market agricultural produce.

Aquaponic Food Production

Learn sustainable farming by combining fish farming and hydroponics, maintenance, water quality, and safety..

Organic Agriculture Production NC II

Focuses on producing organic farm products and creating fertilizers and extracts, adhering to sustainable practices.

Agricultural Crops Production NC II

Develops competencies in cultivating various crops, including nursery operations, crop care and maintenance, harvest and post-harvest procedures.

Covers all aspects of fruit cultivation, from site preparation and growing seedlings to monitoring plant growth and effective harvesting techniques.

Purpose of AaniMo Seminars

AniMo Seminars aim to educate and empower farmers, buyers, and agri-entrepreneurs by offering practical knowledge, expert insights, and the latest updates in agriculture. These seminars help improve farming practices, increase productivity, and support business growth in the agri-sector.
